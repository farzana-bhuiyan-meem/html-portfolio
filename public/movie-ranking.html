My top 3 movies of all-time.

Spirited Away

This is my favourite anime. I love how it evokes a unique mix of wonder and anxiety, along with a profound sense of growth and calmness after overcoming fear, like a dream that is both scary and beautiful.

shawshank Redemption

A masterpiece that evokes a profound sense of enduring hope and ultimate vindication through perseverance against systemic injustice.

Rush Hour

A delightful movie featuring the dynamic comedic chemistry and spectacular action sequences of an unlikely pair.
